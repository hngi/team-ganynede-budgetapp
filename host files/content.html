Why SpendLess?

We calculate your average spending by category, so you can easily create a budget based on spending patterns. This enables you to see how much you are spending on what, on a daily basis or month-to-month.

Determine your budget goals:

Plan ahead and see how much you will save cutting back in any category. You can plan for one-time-only expenses and recurring monthly expenses.

Managing Expenses for you:

We’ll show you exactly how your spending decisions affect the money you have at the end of the month. This will enable you to plan your expenses and save more for tomorrow.




How it works

SpendLess uses budget categories to help you track your spending. It shows you the summary of your expenditures, how much you've saved based on each category and expenses you have made on each category.

Tracking all your bills on the go.

SpendLess track all your bills on the go from credit card, utility bills, data top-up and your insurance. It’s all in one place and easy to manage, reminds you when to carry out a transaction and gives you alerts if funds are low.
