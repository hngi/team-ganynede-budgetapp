About Us

Perfect, money management

The idea behind BudgetIt is that we need the best tool to manage our personal finances. Some people use Microsoft Excel, others use paper and pen, and some use personal accounting software but also, most of us just don’t know what to do and how to go about it. Our financial lives are complicated, cards, cash, savings, debt and we are not all born blessed accountants.
We kicked-off on a journey to solve financial problems. Hence BudgetIt was born a simple and perfect tool to manage your personal finances. BudgetIt wants to offer you an all round view of your money, what you are receiving, what you are spending, what you’ve saved. BudgetIt wants to give you the best tools to understand where your money goes, and enable you to set and achieve your financial aims.
